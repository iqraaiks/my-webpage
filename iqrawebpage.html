Research
My current research involves the conversion of methane to aromatics using zeolite supported metal catalyst, under the supervision of Prof. Kamal K. Pant and Prof. M. Ali Haider .

Natural gas (methane) is an alternative to petroleum for the production of chemicals and other clean liquid fuels. Direct non-oxidative methane conversion into aromatic hydrocarbons is an excellent approach to exploit the natural gas.
